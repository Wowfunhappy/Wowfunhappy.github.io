Useful little Mac Quick Actions

Quick Actions—also sometimes called "Services"—are a way to add bits of functionality to existing Mac apps. Here are some quick actions I've made!

Open In Zoom:

  • Open Zoom links without jumping through a web browser.
  • Useful for joining meetings from the Calendar app!
  • To use, highlight a Zoom URL and in the context (right-click) menu, select Open In Zoom or Services → Open In Zoom.

Download Video:

  • Download Youtube videos from your web browser.
  • Works with Safari, Firefox, Chrome, and Chromium as of March 2021.
  • To use, navigate to a page with a video in your web browser, and activate the service. Select
    • Safari/Firefox/Chrome → Services → Download Video in the menu bar, or assign the Service to a keyboard shortcut in System Preferences!

Finder Quick Actions:

  • Create New Text Document:
    • The equivalent of Windows's "New Text Document" for Finder on your Mac.
    • To use, open Finder and in the menu bar select Finder → Services → New Text Document.
  • Duplicate Finder Window:
    • The equivalent of Windows's "New Text Document" for Finder on your Mac.
    • To use, open Finder and in the menu bar select Finder → Services → Duplicate Finder Window.
  • Make DMG:
    • Quickly create DMGs which contain your files or folders.
    • To use, highlight items in Finder and in the context (right-click) menu, select Make DMG or Services → Make DMG.
  • Reset Finder Window Size:
    • Restore the size of a Finder window to your Mac's default. For when you resize a window accidentally and can no longer get it looking right.
    • To use, open Finder and in the menu bar select Finder → Services → Reset Finder Window Size.
