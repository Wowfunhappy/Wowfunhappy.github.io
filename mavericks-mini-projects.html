Mavericks Mini Projects

Forks, custom builds, and other small projects designed for OS X 10.9 Mavericks.

Below is a set of small projects for my favorite operating system, OS X 10.9 Mavericks. Some are custom builds of existing open source software; others are fully original creations which did not deserve their own page.

Some downloads may also work on other versions of OS X from around the same era, but this is mostly untested.

Audio & Video

QuickTime 10.2 for Mavericks

Mavericks ships with QuickTime Player 10.3, which is incompatible with third-party codecs. Replace it with this modified vesion of QuickTime 10.2 from Mountain Lion.

QuickTime Player is the property of Apple. This modified version is distributed in a good-faith attempt to help Apple customers.

Download

FFusion 2021

A component for QuickTime 10.2 which adds compatibilty with a variety of non-Apple video codecs including HEVC and VP9. Requires a powerful CPU!

Based on RJVB's version of FFusion, which was based on the Perian Project's version of FFusion, which was based on the original by Jérôme Cornen. Also incorporates code by MaddTheSane.

Download

QuickTime Helper

Helps QuickTime open video containers it can't handle natively, such as .mkv files, by remuxing them into a temporary format. Use alongside the above two projects for best results.

App icon by Princess Cake.

Download

Preview Helper

Helps Preview open HEIC, WebP, and BPG images, by creating a temporary copy in the background.

Includes and relies on QuickLook generators by maxpozdeev, dethi, and emin.

Download

Media Subscriptions Preference Pane

Your favorite podcast episodes and Youtube videos, automatically delivered to your Mac's Music and Movies folders. No app required, just add feeds and channels in System Preferences.

Download

Miscellaneous

Apple Mail Security Fix

A plugin for Apple Mail which fixes CVE-2020-9922, a scary zero-click vulnerability.

Download

SIMBL for Mavericks

A Mavericks-compatible version of SIMBL, a plugin system for Mac apps. A few of my favorite plugins are included.

Download

Snow Leopard Window Controls

Replace the small "traffic light" window buttons in Mavericks with the larger ones from Snow Leopard.

Download

Side Button Enabler

Use the side buttons on your mouse to navigate forwards and backwards.

Based on Sensible Side Buttons by Aarchagon.

Download

CodeRunner Mavericks Workarounds

Use Coderunner 3.1 in Mavericks without constant crashes.

CodeRunner, a paid application by Nikolai Krill, must be downloaded seperately.

Download

Gaming

OpenEmu Lite

A Mac-native emulator for NES, SNES, NDS and all flavors of Gameboy games. Based on OpenEmu, but with a stripped-down interface and no library functionality, so you can open a game in Finder and start playing right away.

OpenEmu is to iTunes as OpenEmu Lite is to QuickTime.

Download

Dolphin for Mavericks

A custom, Mavericks-compatible build of the Dolphin Wii and Gamecube emulator.

Based on Dolphin v5.0-14357
from June 6, 2021

Download

Godot

A custom, Mavericks-compatible build of the Godot game engine.

Based on Godot v3.3.2
